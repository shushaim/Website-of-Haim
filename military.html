At the age of twenty I joined the IDF, where I had the fortune to serve in several facinating roles.

I started as a flight cadet at the Air Force, and recorded fifteen flight hours on a Piper Super Cub, before replacing my blue Air Force unifom with the green ones of the Armored corps.

I then volunteered to attend Officer School, and, upon graduation, received the rank of 2nd Lieutenant.

I served as a Human Resource Officer and then as a Staff Officer at the Golan Division.

After four years of service, I was honorably discharged at the rank of 1st Lieutenant and was assigned to a reserve unit.
